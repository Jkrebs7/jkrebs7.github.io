  • I would be expected to be experinced in one or more of the following security domains: Threat Management, Data and Application Security, Identity & Access Management, Cloud and Infrastructure Security, and/or Strategy and Risk.
  • I would also be expected to know netwrok design, security, authentication, and encription most likely.
  • Of course I would be expetd to have knowledge on how to code and use software tools related to coding.

Computer/PC Technician

  • This job is probably on the lower end for income but this is mainly due to experince required and the more entry level nature of the position. I tried to focus on careers that were more Beginner focused.
  • A PC Technician is responsible for maintaining, supporting, and optimizing all network related software and opterating systems.
  • Salary according to online sources could range from $50,000 - $64,000 a year for this position. Also depending on current skillset and other factors.
  • Future job potential for this career looks decent, with possible ways to move up and slightly increase salary over time/exp.
  • The dgree you need or is prefered by the employer can vary most of the time. Some employers ask for a CS Degree and others ask for a IT Degree.
  • Employers definitely prefer previous work experience here, such as a couple years of experience installing, configuring, and troubleshooting windows OS. Also knowing the in and outs to computer hardware and software is a plus as well.

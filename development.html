What is a PDP?

A professional devlopment plan is a document that you yourself typically write to reinforce many aspects of you current status in your career or life in general. Professional devlopment plans are commonly used to help people in the workplace reinforce what their long term goals are with their career and how they can improve based on how they assess their performance. It is also a very helpful tool not only for work but life overall, it can help you asses your overall life goals and how you plan to reach them.A professional devlopment plan is a very useful tool that more people should take advantage of whether you're a student or an employee.
